Digital Forensics & Cyber Intelligence (LEA)
New Delhi, Delhi, India

DEEPAK KUMAR (D3) is Cyber Intelligence & Digital Forensics Examiner | Consultant | Trainer has more than 7 years of comprehensive experience in various verticals of the domain. His area of interest is in Digital Forensic, Cyber-Security & Intelligence. He has been working on several consulting engagements for the major Academia, Finance, Governments, and Law Enforcement Agencies & ITES/BFS Enterprise sectors.He is also a highly interactive trainer & consultant, whose work in mainly based on research and applied knowledge from extensive experiences in digital forensics and cyber-security. He has not only assisted in solving complex cyber-crime and forensic cases but has also played an instrumental role in creating awareness about forensics and cyber-crimes. So far he has delivered talks/ guest-lectures/seminars/workshops/training on various domains like ethical-hacking, cyber-crimes/security awareness, cyber-threats & digital forensics. He has delivered workshops and corporate training to IT companies, esteemed institutions, Law enforcement and organizations; and also trained 1500+ high-end professional individually.
